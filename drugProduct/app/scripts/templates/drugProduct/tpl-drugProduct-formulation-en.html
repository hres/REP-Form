• The formulation “ID” is auto generated by the template when you click on “Add Formulation”.
  • Copy button: After saving ingredient / formulation details, you may choose to copy those details into a new ingredient / formulation record and change one or more fields as required. This will help minimize the amount of data entry for similar ingredients / formulations.
  • When an ingredient/material is identified as Animal / Human sourced, you must provide additional details by selecting the “Animal / Human Sourced” tab next to the “Formulations” tab.
