• When a manufacturer transfers the possession and responsibility for a product to another manufacturer. (DIN stays the same)
  • Note: Dossier ID will be transferred from the previous owner if all DINs within the dossier are being transferred. Alternatively, contact Health Canada for more information (eReview@hc-sc.gc.ca).
  • On the RA template: Use company ID for new owner and use existing dossier ID, unless a new dossier ID was assigned by Health Canada.
